# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Everyone who participates in this project — contributors, maintainers, and users — agrees to make participation a welcoming, respectful experience for all, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our Standards

Positive behavior includes:

- Using clear, welcoming language
- Respecting different viewpoints and experience levels
- Accepting constructive feedback graciously
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Personal attacks or derogatory comments
- Public or private conduct that a reasonable person would consider unprofessional
- Sharing others private information without consent

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate action in response to any unacceptable conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including the repository, issue tracker, and any other official communication channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and investigated, and will result in a response deemed appropriate to the circumstances. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality regarding the reporter.
